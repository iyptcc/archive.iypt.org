Collection

The sources are arranged with respect to the year they focus on; books and analytical essays are listed in separate subgroups. There are particular overlaps between the subgroups and further overlaps with the other sections of the Archive. The records are in Russian, Czech, Dutch, English, Hungarian, Slovak, French, Belarusian, Georgian, Ukrainian, Polish, German, Swedish, Croatian, Bulgarian, Portuguese, Korean, Chinese, and other languages.
