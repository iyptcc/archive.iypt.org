People

The extended audience of the IYPT, including participants of national competitions, is at the order of several hundred thousand people.

Among those, there are now over 5000 people who have attended the international event of the IYPT. Relying on the existing sources, this section traces the tournament over a span of 29 years, looking at all of its participants, organizers, and jurors; and bringing together their names.

The names of jurors, organizers, and invited guests provide an insight into the social dynamics of a diverse community that have been bringing IYPT to prominence and success since its foundation. From young enthusiastic undergraduates to Nobel prize winners or Directors General of CERN, they all contributed to the growth of the competition, while each had a unique vision of why the IYPT is extraordinary and needs protection and support.

The IYPT, at the same time, would never reach its true potential without the generations of participants fascinated with physics, communication, international connections, pre-university research, and expanding their minds with new experiences. What is the demographics of the participants, and how they might have changed over the 29 years? What did the entrants go on to do? What are the case studies of winners? What do they say about the IYPT?

A large number of past participants made successful careers in academia or industry, often crediting the IYPT as “a good schooling” or even “a springboard”, but sometimes also sharing nuanced critical feedback about the competition.

Some former participants have completed an undergraduate degree in one and a half years, or promptly brought their still-IYPT project to a mainstream publication.

The IYPT has seen participants who went on to get published in Science or Nature, make front covers, or reach a substantial h-index.

Some past participants went on to become indie movie directors, top executives in major banks, or CEO at companies with $120M revenue. Surprisingly, the IYPT has even seen a team leader who went on to become a National prime minister.

The names of the participants speak for the excellence and success of many individuals who proudly keep the IYPT on their professional CVs. As 30 years passed from the earliest IYPTs, we may look if in the long run the experience has influenced on their professional future.

When no single past participant or juror is forgotten, the community can better understand the long-term educational impact and relevance of the IYPT, foster the continuity, and recognize the accomplishments and highlights of the IYPT in a retrospective. This strengthens the sense of connection, shows the trends, and reveals some vibrant stories of success.
